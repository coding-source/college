Facilities

Classrooms

The Classrooms at CIET are spacious and well furnished. It provides a blend of traditional learning methods, which enables students to learn what they need, when they need it, with the ability to put that knowledge and skills to use effectively.

EXPLORE

Library

To promote a spirit of continuous learning among its students, Coimbatore Institute of Engineering and Technology assures peaceful and enriching hours in the Central Library. One of the major highlights of the Coimbatore Institute of Engineering and Technology is its spacious central library that has a seating capacity of 200. It has 40796 volumes under 21179 titles of books under 9329 E-books. The library also subscribes 150 printed Journals, 4239 E-Journals and 30 Magazines. In this fully automated library, students and staff members can search the books by author, title, accession number, publisher or subject heading and their availability. Each student is given four library books at a time, and they have allocated library hours/ week. The Coimbatore Institute of Engineering and Technology Central Library is an institutional member of prestigious libraries such as the DELNET, AICTE-ELSEVIER, and AICTE-IEEE Delhi. In addition to this, the library also has NPTEL resources.

Hostel

Separate Hostel facilities are available for both Boys and Girls.
The hostel rooms are spacious with natural light and ventilation. Each student is provided with a study table, chair. Each hostel has 24 hour electricity and water supply, water cooler, common telephone, and common TV room..

Sports

Sports is an integral part of our college. “Health is wealth” those who have understood its importance will try to keep themselves fit. CIET initiates sports activities for maintaining health and physical fitness. Students have the facilities of both indoor and outdoor sports..

Transportation

The college has a fleet of luxury buses that ply on several routes across Coimbatore and its neighbouring areas, serving both students and staff.

Conference Hall

The conference hall hosts a number of placement interviews, where in students from various colleges assemble to attend seminars and group discussions. NPTEL video courses, IIT Remote center programmes, Online courses, Guest lectures, project presentations, science exhibitions and paper presentations are held in the conference halls.

ABOUT CIET

Coimbatore Institute of Engineering and Technology offers the students with an advantageous atmosphere with state-of-the-art facilities, distinguished mentors, and pleasant educational environment. The institution provides employability and communication skills for the development of students. It provides quality education in an atmosphere of discipline. The focus is on shaping students to become self-disciplined, self-dependent and self-confident individuals. Coimbatore Institute of Engineering and Technology pulls out all the stops to mould the students’ career in such a way that they excel in all subtle distinction of life. The College’s stand is not only on the mere acquisition of course knowledge and its application but also on all-round personality development of the student and his value system. The infrastructure facilities have also been made to allow detailed learning ambience for the students. The full-fledged eco-friendly offers a quality environment to the students
